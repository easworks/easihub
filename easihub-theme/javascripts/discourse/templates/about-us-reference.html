Our Journey: From Vision to Enterprise Application Community

In 2023, we recognized a critical gap in the enterprise application ecosystem. While countless forums existed for general programming, enterprise application professionals — architects, developers, consultants, and system integrators — lacked a unified platform designed for their unique challenges in ERP, CRM, PLM, SCM, HCM, BI/Analytics, and cloud platform implementation.

What if we could create a platform where EA professionals could truly connect, learn, and grow together?

This question sparked the creation of EASIHUB for community-driven learning and collaboration. As our community grew, we also launched EASWORKS to help our members find career opportunities.

Today, EASIHUB serves as the primary hub for enterprise application professionals to connect, learn, and grow together, while EASWORKS helps bridge the gap between our talented community members and companies seeking EA expertise.
